Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services

What counts as an endpoint?
An endpoint is any device protected by our EDR agent, including laptops, desktops, servers, and virtual machines. Each device running our agent software counts as one endpoint.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?
We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan. No credit card required to start your trial.
What support options are included?
Starter includes email support, Professional includes priority support with faster response times, and Enterprise includes 24/7 premium support with a dedicated account manager.
Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?
Yes, we offer custom enterprise solutions for large organizations with specific requirements. Contact our sales team for more information.
