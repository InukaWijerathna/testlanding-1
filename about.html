Our Story

Techzaz EDR is a cloud-hosted Endpoint Detection and Response platform designed to help companies and individuals protect their infrastructures, endpoints, and cloud assets from modern cyber threats.

Our solution provides real-time threat monitoring, incident detection, threat intelligence integration, and response automation through a centralized dashboard. It enables registered users to manage their endpoints, view telemetry data, detect suspicious behavior, and respond to security incidents effectively.

With a focus on data privacy and compliance (ISO 27001, GDPR-friendly), our modular architecture is suitable for both academic and commercial deployment.
